ABOUT US

Welcome to Eco Tech – Your ultimate hub for creativity, inspiration, and endless possibilities! Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or just starting your DIY journey, we’re here to provide you with the tools, tutorials, and community support to help you create something amazing. Join us in turning everyday items into extraordinary creations. Let’s craft a better world together, one project at a time.



Mission

Our website, Eco Tech's mission is to empower users by providing a wide range of DIY (Do-It-Yourself) projects and resources. We aim to inspire creativity and self-sufficiency, offering step-by-step guides, tips, and tutorials that make it easy for anyone to tackle their own projects and bring their ideas to life. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned DIY enthusiast, our platform is here to support you in every creative endeavor.


Vision

Eco Tech is to inspire and empower individuals to create, innovate, and personalize their world through accessible, engaging, and sustainable DIY projects. We envision a community where everyone can explore their creativity, learn new skills, and share their unique creations with pride.


Values

At Eco Tech, we value creativity, accessibility, and sustainability. We offer easy-to-follow resources for all skill levels, use eco-friendly materials, and foster a supportive community. We're committed to quality, continuous learning, and sharing our passion for DIY.


How we started

In 2022, online friends from different towns , Myanmar, were determined to solve the issue of finding quality, affordable home appliances. Using their skills they started working in a small garage. Despite many challenges, they created DIY website, Eco Tech, and launched it in 2024. The website quickly became popular in their community and beyond, setting the foundation for their growing business.
